Introduction

Hi, My name is Miles!

I hope that you enjoy learning about who I am. Please browse all pages and information.

I was born in Modesto, California in 2001. I grew up in San Jose, California for the majority where I discovered my love of sports, music and travelling. Throughout my life I have played both hockey and the drums which may seem like an interesting pairing but these outlets enabled me to better understand myself and my community as a whole!

Early Life

Heart Disease

When I was born, I had a congenital heart defect named," Anomalous Left Coronary Artery from the Pulmonary Artery." (ALCAPA)

I needed to have surgery at only 2 months old, a very risky surgery. After a completely successful surgery the doctor's predicted that I will develop slowly, be underweight, and be mostly smaller than most of my peers. I am happy to say that they were wrong as I stand 6'0" tall and stocky. I have always had to keep in mind this setback but I have been able to have an appreciative view on life because of it.

Early Life

Modesto

Life in Modesto was fun, just very hot.

The best way to describe Modesto is like going to Las Vegas, just minus the casinos, the hotels, the people, and pretty much all of the enterainment. I know I may not be selling the city but as a child I had a blast, housing is relatively cheap so my family was able to have a pool in our backyard. I remember summers learning how to swim in it and even pool parties with neighbors.

Hobbies and Interests

Hockey

After moving to San Jose I began getting interested in hockey and spent all of my childhood playing!

A fun fact about San Jose is that it is home to the San Jose Sharks! Because this hockey team calls San Jose home, the surrounding area has a multitude of leagues and teams for everyone to join. Although I was never part of a travel team, I always had this outlet to blow off steam and learn an exciting skill.

Hobbies and Interests

Drumming

"Not quite my tempo." -J.K. Simmons (Whiplash)

I began playing the drums in the 3rd grade. After seeing that the program for my school was lack luster I began taking private lessons and learning about this great instrument. I played in the jazz band all throughout middle school and high school which allowed me to discover my love for music in a multitude of genres.

Michigan State University

Friends

I did not expect to be at Michigan State when planning on going to college but I am happy I picked it.

My first choice in a university was Univesity of Southern California. I was not admitted however after being accepted to Michigan State I was unsure of if I was going to stay in California or travel out of state. After visiting the campus and understanding the sheer size and complexity to MSU i fell in love with it. I knew I wanted a large school with many different opportunities and communities for me to explore.

Family

My Family

Whether I am near or far my family is always there for me

As I am about to graduate, I relfect on my life so far and I can not help but realize that my family has been the most helpful,supportive, and kind force in my life. They have been there for me in times of need and have always listened and respected my opinions. They have let me carve my own life and have my own opinions on matters while also challeneging my thoughts with critical thinking and emotional intelligence. They will alwyas be there for me and I will always be there for them.
